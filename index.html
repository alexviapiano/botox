The Botox Experience at Clinic Botox

  • Forehead lines

    Botox is used to smooth horizontal wrinkles across the forehead, which are caused by raising the eyebrows.

  • Glabellar lines

    Also known as "11 lines," these are treated to reduce the appearance of vertical frown lines between the eyebrows.

  • Crow's feet

    Botox injections at the outer corners of the eyes diminish the fine lines that appear when smiling or squinting.

  • Bunny lines

    These are the wrinkles on the bridge of the nose that Botox can soften when the muscles scrunch up, such as when laughing or frowning.

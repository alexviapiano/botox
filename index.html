Personalized Botox Solutions

Nurse led personalized Botox treatments tailored to your individual needs and aesthetic goals. From smoothing wrinkles to enhancing facial contours, we ensure natural and stunning results.

What is a neuromodulator?

Injecting neuromodulators like Botox, Dysport, Xeomin, or Nuceiva blocks muscle contractions, helping prevent wrinkle formation. The treatment involves a fine needle delivering the product into targeted muscles. In the initial consultation, we'll determine the appropriate dosage for your desired outcome. The effects start appearing within 4 to 7 days, with full results visible after 14 days. The results typically last between 3 to 4 months.

What is a filler?

Hyaluronic acid, a natural component of our skin, aids in hydration and supports fatty structures. With age, its regeneration diminishes, leading to less hydrated tissues and decreased facial volume. It can be injected into various areas to address these issues, including under-eye dark circles, lips, cheekbones, jawline, chin, nose, wrinkles, fine lines, nasolabial folds, and temples.

The Botox Experience

  • Forehead lines

    Botox is used to smooth horizontal wrinkles across the forehead, which are caused by raising the eyebrows.

  • Glabellar lines

    Also known as "11 lines," these are treated to reduce the appearance of vertical frown lines between the eyebrows.

  • Crow's feet

    Botox injections at the outer corners of the eyes diminish the fine lines that appear when smiling or squinting.

  • Bunny lines

    These are the wrinkles on the bridge of the nose that Botox can soften when the muscles scrunch up, such as when laughing or frowning.
